SCORING ENGINE

Real-time LPMAMA
lead scoring.

Six qualification dimensions, drawn from the real estate industry's standard LPMAMA framework, evaluated continuously and rolled into one live score.

L

Location

Specific neighborhood preference, or open to anything — certainty scores higher.

P

Price

A narrow, certain budget scores far higher than a wide, unsure one.

M

Motivation

Relocating next month, or casually browsing — urgency is one of the strongest signals.

A

Agent status

Already working with another agent, or a real open opportunity.

M

Mortgage

Cash buyer or pre-approved moves fast; not-yet-started weighs heavily the other way.

A

Appointment

A booked viewing is the single strongest signal in the score.

Cold0–24 Cool25–49 Warm50–74 Hot75–100
This is a live, explainable, rules-based algorithm — not a generative-AI guess. Every score comes with a reason for each dimension, and the weights are tunable as we learn what actually predicts a close in your market.
